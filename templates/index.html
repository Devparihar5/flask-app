About our app

Tool which is able to horizontally crawl and gather data across different platforms. This search could be based upon mail ID or the mobile number. Expectation: Police will be able to crowd source the information from different platforms and instantly develop the profile of the suspected person. eg- based upon different types of classification. Result (expected): Police will get a handy tool which will be able to generate digital profile of any suspect/criminal within minutes.
